### Quick Techniques

* **Viber contact enrichment:** If you add a phone number to your contacts and it appears in Viber, you may see additional information (e.g., profile name, photo). Use responsibly and in compliance with local laws and platform policies.
* **Instagram recovery hinting:** Initiating a password reset with an email/username can reveal masked contact hints (e.g., partial phone digits). These can corroborate identities. Do **not** attempt unauthorized access; use only for correlation/verification.
