<ExpandableSection title="AI Summary">
This talk, presented at VMware Explore 2022 in San Francisco, demonstrates how to bridge the gap between standardized threat intelligence feeds and VMware Carbon Black Cloud's proprietary format. The session introduces a purpose-built tool that ingests third-party threat intelligence data -- such as STIX/TAXII feeds, IOC databases, and threat reports -- and converts it into a format that Carbon Black Cloud can consume for detection and response. The tool automates what was previously a manual and error-prone process, enabling security teams to operationalize external threat intelligence directly within their Carbon Black Cloud deployment.
</ExpandableSection>
